Remember. if you roll the value of your LU or higher, you score a Critical Hit!
(You must have at least 1 LU to be able to score Critical Hits.)

Hitting an enemy with a Critical Hit or an Element they are weak to will cause them to be knocked down! Doing this gets you a One More, which allows you to gain another turn. Enemies can get One Mores too!

In a One More, you can take another turn, or you can Baton Pass it to an ally! An ally under the effects of a Baton Pass does +2 extra damage--and if they get a One More, they can Baton Pass again!
If the whole team gets a Baton Pass, you automatically win a non-boss combat!

If all enemies in a combat are knocked down, then you get a Hold-Up! In a Hold-Up, you can use your Social Stats to negotiate for money or items, or you can launch an All-Out Attack, in which you and all of your allies score an automatic hit with your melee attack and deal Almighty Damage.
